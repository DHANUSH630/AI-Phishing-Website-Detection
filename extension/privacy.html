Official Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

🔒 Privacy First & Zero-Server Policy

AI Phishing Shield was designed from the ground up as a privacy-first utility. We believe that securing your browsing activity should not involve tracking it. 100% of the analysis, scanning, and machine learning evaluations take place locally on your device.

🌐 Does any data leave your browser?

No External Transmissions

Absolutely no data leaves your browser. Unlike traditional link-checkers that upload the URLs you visit to cloud-based security lists, AI Phishing Shield performs all computations offline:

  • No URL Uploads: The URLs you visit are parsed and analyzed entirely within the extension service worker.
  • Local AI Model: TensorFlow.js neural network inference and fallback scoring are executed client-side inside your browser sandbox.
  • No Cloud Dependencies: The extension operates fully without calling any third-party APIs.

📂 What data do we access?

To protect you from phishing attempts, the extension temporarily reads the following information when a page loads:

  • Tab URLs: To extract heuristic patterns (length, TLD, subdomain depth, typosquatting checking).
  • Page DOM Elements: The content script scans page input forms to verify if login fields are posting securely, if hidden elements are present, or if copy/right-click actions are hijacked. This data is read in-memory and discarded immediately after the scan.

💾 What data is stored locally?

Any persistent data is saved inside Chrome's secure local sandbox storage and is never shared. This includes:

  • Scan History Audit: A list of your previously visited pages, their calculated phishing scores, and threat flags.
  • Statistics: Aggregated numbers (e.g., total sites scanned, safe vs blocked sites).
  • Allow & Block Lists: Custom domain rules you manually create to bypass or enforce warnings.
  • Extension Settings: Your configured preferences (e.g., enable/disable badge, warn thresholds).

Note: You can purge all of this data instantly at any time by clicking "Clear History" in the popup settings or web dashboard.

📊 Telemetry & Analytics

Zero Tracking

We do not collect telemetry, crash logs, diagnostic statistics, or usage habits. There are no tracking scripts, analytics modules, or advertisements bundled within the extension package.
